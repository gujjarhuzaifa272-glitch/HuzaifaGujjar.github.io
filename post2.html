Introduction to AI chatbots and automations

Published November 20, 2025

Artificial intelligence (AI) has become increasingly accessible to developers and businesses of all sizes. One of the most practical applications is the use of AI‑powered chatbots and workflow automations to streamline customer support, sales and internal operations.

To build a chatbot, you’ll need to choose a platform or API for natural language processing (NLP), define your conversation flow and integrate it with your data or services. Popular options include OpenAI’s GPT models, Google Dialogflow and Microsoft Bot Framework.

Workflow automations often involve connecting different services—like email, CRM systems and databases—to trigger actions based on predefined rules. Tools such as Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat) and custom scripts can help you automate repetitive tasks and save time.

In future articles, I’ll share code examples and step‑by‑step guides to help you create your own chatbots and automations. If you have a project in mind or would like personalised assistance, feel free to reach out.
